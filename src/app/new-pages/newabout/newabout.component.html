About empower

Based on the idea of using technology to enable a circular economy and create a cleaner and better world, Empower was founded in January 2018. We are building a global plastic waste ecosystem on the same philosophy as the Norwegian bottle deposit system. By rewarding deposit of plastic waste we can both stop leakage of plastic into the environment, and incentivise collection of leaked waste. The use of Blockchain technology ensures full transparency
