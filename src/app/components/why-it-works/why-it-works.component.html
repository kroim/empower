why it works

A cleaner world
We believe that, with mutual efforts, we can achieve a better, cleaner, healthier environment worldwide, and help fight poverty while doing it.
Incentivized Cleaning
Through the use of blockchain technology, we’re able to instantly reward a digital token worth 1 dollar for every 1 kilogram of plastic waste brought to us, enabling those in need to earn money by being a part of this new global eco-community.
Everyone can help
Becoming part of this community is easy – we receive funding on a subscription basis, allowing everyone to choose exactly how much they want to help!
Effecient impact
For every one dollar you donate, one kilogram of harmful plastic waste will be recycled and removed from the environment, allowing you to become a part of the solution, instead of being a part of the problem.
