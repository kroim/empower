Plastic
Plastic pollution is the world's fastest growing environmental challenge. Every minute 15 tonnes of plastic reaches the ocean from land based sources. Empower rewards plastic waste cleanups with EMP Tokens.
Poverty
At least 765 million people live of less than 1.90 USD per day. Empower allows people to lift themselves from poverty by reducing plastic waste.
Empower
Our EMP Token gives value to plastic waste by using blockchain technology. This will Empower people to take control of their own lives and save the planet at the same time.
