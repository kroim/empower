do you want to help save the planet?

Whether you see it with your own eyes or not, plastic waste is destroying our oceans and beautiful nature.

Plastic is a material that the Earth cannot digest. Every piece of plastic ever produced is still here with us and will be for hundreds of years.

Even something as little as throwing out a plastic bottle leads to it breaking down to smaller and smaller pieces, attracting toxic chemicals, and contaminating nature around you.

We aim to solve this problem by creating a fund that rewards people for bringing their unwanted plastic waste to any of our certified recycling stations.

how it works

step 1

Anyone can gather plastic waste

step 2

The waste is delivered at a certified recycling station

step 3

1 EMP token is rewarded for each kilogram of plastic waste (1 EMP always equals $1)

step 4

Tokens can be used in the ecosystem or sold back for local currency
